Add playlist!

Here you can paste a URI to add your own playlists to the Nobelnet user.
This makes it easier and faster to add your own music in the bar!

How?

  1. Open your own Spotify
  2. Find a playlist you want to add
  3. Right click and select 'Copy URI'
  4. Paste into the field above and click 'Go!'
